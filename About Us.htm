About Us

EGGEAT is a New York food guide for all the foodies out there. EGGEAT's founder, Denise Ho, once interned in New York and fell in love with everything about New York. Her mission is to create a trustworthy platform for people who are travelling to New York to decide their restaurants within 30 seconds.
